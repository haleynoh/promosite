About Moulin Rouge!

Moulin Rouge is a Broadway musical based on the 2001 jukebox musical romantic drama film produced by Baz Luhrman. The first performace was July 10, 2018, and it has won big titles such as Tony Award for Best Musical and Tony Award for Best Orchestrations. It is now showing at the Al Hirschfield Theater, 302 W 45th St New York, NY.

Storyline

A young Englishman in the Paris of 1899, becomes infatuated with Satine, a singer at the Moulin Rouge. However, she has been promised by the manager to a Duke in return for funding his next production. As the young lovers meet in secret, Satine's wedding day draws closer but she hides a fatal secret from both Christian and the Duke.
